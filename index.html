About Us

EasyFix Solutions is a trusted manpower and service provider company headquartered in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. Since our inception, we have been committed to helping businesses operate smoothly by supplying reliable workforce and technical experts across multiple industries. We pride ourselves on our ability to provide the right people for the right job at the right time.Our manpower services include skilled, semi-skilled and unskilled categories such as Security Guards, Housekeeping Staff, Pantry Boys, Supervisors, and more. On the technical side, we provide experienced Electricians, Plumbers, Painters, Welders, and Fitters who can deliver both short-term and long-term support depending on client requirements.
