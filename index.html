About EasyFix Solutions

EasyFix Solutions is a trusted manpower and service provider headquartered in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. Since inception we supply trained manpower and on-site technical experts — ensuring quick response, statutory compliance (PF, ESIC, Bonus) and reliable service delivery across residential and commercial customers.

  • Skilled & semi-skilled manpower (security, housekeeping, pantry)
  • Technical teams: electricians, plumbers, painters, welders, fitters
  • Flexible contracts: one-time, short-term & long-term
